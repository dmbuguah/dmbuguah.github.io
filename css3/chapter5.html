Fonts are loaded as external assets and must be downloaded by the browser before they can be displayed. Before the file has loaded, no font at all will be visible on the elements the font is to be applied to. You may then see a slight flash that occurs as the font loads and is applied. This is known as the Flash of Unstyled Text (FoUT).
